About Tamil Calendar

The Tamil calendar is a sidereal solar calendar used in Tamil Nadu. The Tamil year starts with the month of Chithirai (mid-April). The calendar follows the Thiruvalluvar year (திருவள்ளுவர் ஆண்டு), which is approximately 31 years ahead of the Gregorian calendar. The current conversion uses this dating system.
