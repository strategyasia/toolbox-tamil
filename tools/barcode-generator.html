Barcode Format Information

CODE128: Alphanumeric, compact, widely used in logistics and retail. Supports uppercase, lowercase, numbers and special characters.

CODE39: Alphanumeric, self-checking. Supports 43 different characters including numbers, letters, and limited punctuation.

EAN13: 13-digit numeric barcode used for retail products. Standard format for product identification.

UPC: Universal Product Code, 12-digit numeric barcode. Commonly used on retail products in North America.

QR Code: 2D barcode that can store more data. Can encode URLs, contact information, and text.
